Jamaica

Jamaica is a country on an island in the carribean sea. Jamaica is apart of the British Commonwealth, and has been since the 17th century. It is a tropical island populated with beaches and river. The land of rum and lumber has a beautiful mountain range called the blue mountains. Jamaica is the perfect place to relax on the beach with a glass of rum to watch a beautiful sunset. Come take an escape and see the Dunn's River falls or enjoy biking down the mountains.

  • Dunn's River Falls

    Ochos Rios, Jamaica

    Dunn's River Falls is a waterfall apart of the Dunn's River in Ocho Rios. It is a well known tourist attraction, and a the end of the river which open up into the Carribean Sea. Take a trip from the city to the falls with a tour guide, from Viator.

    $38.99

  • Blue Mountains

    Kingston, Jamaica

    The largest range in Jamaica, and the highest peak in the country, the blue mountains are a beautiful natural experience. From the peak, which is accessible from a path, you can see the outline of Cuba. Take a bike tour down the mountain, with the help of Viator.

    $92.99

  • Doctor's Cave Beach

    Montego bay, Jamaica

    One of the most famous beaches, Doctor's Cave Beach has been a site to enjoy your on the ocean's edge for centuries. It once was a small private beach for Dr. McCatty and his friends. The beach is now open to the public for $5, and has snorkel gear, umbrellas, and chairs to rent. It is close to a few hotels as well. Get ticket for an excursion to go snorkeling and hit the hot spots on the beach.

    $27.99
