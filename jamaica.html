Jamaica

Jamaica is an island in the Caribbean sea, it is a part of the British Commonwealth, and has been since the 17th century. The land of rum and lumber has a beautiful mountain range called the blue mountains. Jamaica is the perfect place to relax on the beach with a glass of rum to watch the beautiful sunset.

  • Doctor's Cave Beach

    Montego Bay, Jamaica

    One of the most famous beaches, Doctor's Cave Beach has been a site to enjoy on the ocean's edge for centuries. It once was a small private beach for Dr. McCatty and his friends. The beach is now open to the public, and has snorkel gear, umbrellas, and chairs to rent. It is close to a few hotels.

    $30.00
