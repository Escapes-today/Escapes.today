Jamaica

Jamaica is a country on an island in the carribean sea. Jamaica is apart of the British Commonwealth, and has been since the 17th century. It is a tropical island populated with beaches and river. The land of rum and lumber has a beautiful mountain range called the blue mountains. Jamaica is the perfect place to relax on the beach with a glass of rum to watch a beautiful sunset. Come take an escape and see the Dunn's River falls or enjoy biking down the mountains.
