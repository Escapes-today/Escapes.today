Japan

Japan, a constitutional monarchy, is a group of islands in East Asia. Populated by ancient temples and ruins that show a glimpse into the past of the ancient culture Japan once had. In the heart of the country, Tokyo, the change of ancient to modern is appearant. A city that doesn't sleep, Tokyo is a technological giant, yet still have the culture of Japan flowing through it. Japan is a country full of sites to see, from cultural, to natural, to even technological.

  • Mount Fuji

    Tokyo, Japan

    Seen from Tokyo, Mt. Fuji is one of the three holy mountains of Japan. One of Japan's most famous cultural and natural landmarks, the mountain has played an important role on the country since ancient times. The mountain is also a volcano, but hasn't erupted since the 18th century.     Kamakura, Japan

    Kōtoku-in is a Buddhist Temple in the Kanagawa prefuncture. The statue is made entirely of bronze and is one of Japan's most Iconic structures. The statue most likely dates to the 13th century.
