Japan

Japan, is a group of islands in East Asia. Populated by ancient temples and ruins that show a glimpse into the past of Japan. Located in the heart of the country, Tokyo, the change in architecture from ancient to modern is apparent. A city that doesn't sleep, Tokyo is a technological giant, yet still has the culture of Japan flowing through it. Japan is a country full of sites to see, from cultural, to natural, to even technological.

  • Mount Fuji Bike Tour

    Tokyo, Japan

    Mt. Fuji is one of the three holy mountains of Japan. One of Japan's most famous cultural and natural landmarks, this mountain has played an important role in the country since ancient times. The mountain is also a volcano, but hasn't erupted since the 18th century. There are excursions where you can bike around Lake Kawaguchi, the second largest lake.

    $150.00

  • Kōtoku-in Tour

    Kamakura, Japan

    Kōtoku-in is a Buddhist Temple in the Kanagawa Prefecture. The statue is made entirely of bronze and is one of Japan's most iconic structures. The statue most likely dates to the 13th century. You can take a look at the hall, which has been damaged and renovated time and time again. With a day long trip, you'll see the giant statue and many other ancient temples and ruins.

    $100.00
